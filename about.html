About Pray Here

"Pray Here" was created for our Muslim siblings to locate prayer spaces (mosque and musollah) nearby anywhere in the world. This application was created by me, motivated to make prayer locations known to users within their area at that time. As I am based in Singapore, the first locations included would be in that region. Please feel free to share any prayer areas you may know of in the world!

This app hopes to bring Muslims together by supporting access to prayer areas. It is my hope that each person can perform their prayers in a more comfortable space.

While using any of the prayer areas, let us all be mindful of our behaviour and cleanliness. Keep the prayer areas in its original state or better. Let us all show graciousness and best characters as a Muslim.
