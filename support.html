Support

Do support by submitting prayer areas that you know of, and ideally one that you have used before. You can do so by submitting through the Google Form. You can refer to the documentation to further understand what each section entails.

Additionally, I am open to donations if your rizq allows and you have spare change around. It's a "Buy Me A Coffee" application which you can find the icon at the bottom right. There is a suggested amount but you can ignore that. I would be thankful for any amount that comes.

Suggestions and Reports

If you have any suggestions or find any issues related to the application, feel free to submit them via the Google form as well. Please be as descriptive as possible when submitting them. As for issues, let me know what were the actions you made before the bug came about so that I can re-create the scenario.
